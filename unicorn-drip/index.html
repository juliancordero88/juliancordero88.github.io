Why Us

The Magic

Fourth-Generation Craft

Our roasting partner Jason Richter is a fourth-generation coffee roaster. That's over a century of knowledge poured into every bag — combined with the most advanced roasting technology available today.

Freshness Guaranteed

We roast 4–5 days a week. Your coffee ships within hours of roasting — never sitting on a warehouse shelf for months. Fresh is the difference between good coffee and great coffee.

Specialty Grade Only

If a bean doesn't hit our flavor threshold, it doesn't get roasted. Period. Every coffee in our lineup has been rigorously cupped and selected for clarity, balance, and exceptional taste.

Small Batch, Big Flavor

Using the Loring S15 roaster — recirculating hot-air technology that produces clean, consistent batches with remarkable precision. The kind of technology that lets craft and science agree.

Our Story

Rare. Bold. Magical.

Unicorn Drip Coffee was built on a simple idea: the best coffee in the world shouldn't be hard to find. Drip is how we brew it. Unicorn is how rare and special it should feel.

We partner exclusively with specialty roasters who share our obsession with quality — because when you pour that first cup in the morning, it should feel like something worth waking up for.

No filler. No fluff. Just mythically good coffee, delivered fresh to your door.
